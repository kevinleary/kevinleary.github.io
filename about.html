About Me

Education, Career, Goals, and Life

Early Life and Education

I grew up in a quiet Suburb of Northern New Jersey in a house filled with Technology. My father was a a programmer and so he had blessed (or cursed us) with technology at a very young age. I started programming when I was 15 because of my love for videogames. I had wanted to develop my own.

I created my own class with some tech-minded buddies and we made 2 games over the course of the school year. It was awesome to release a product that we created from scratch and had fun with.

After 3 solid years of introductory Comp Sci courses, I decided to attempt it at university. I attended Florida Atlantic University in Fall 2015 and succesfully completed in Spring 2019.

It was right before I graduated that the US Navy came to my school to recruit. I flew out to Washington DC from South Florida and met with my future coworkers. After 2 years at the Navy creating and progressing their DevSecOps initiative, I moved on to Booz Allen Hamilton. I currently work for DTRA and design The Pipelines that they require.
